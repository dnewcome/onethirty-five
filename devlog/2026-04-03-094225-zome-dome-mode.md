# zome: add dome/full mode, fix default to N=12 φ=30°

_2026-04-03_

## What happened

Added a dome/full toggle so you can view just the upper hemisphere or the complete closed zonohedron. The default was also locked to N=12 at φ=30°, which is the most structurally interesting case — 12 generators produces 132 rhombic faces organized into 11 zone bands, and 30° elevation gives a proportionally pleasing dome. Without a sensible default the tool was a bit disorienting on first load.
